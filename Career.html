Career-Related Resources

These opportunities belong to you!

Virginia Tech provides a multitude of career resources and opportunities that can uplift female leaders and risk-takers. These resources are here to ensure that each and every young woman apart of the VT community has access to  all these useful tools in a convenient space. Please take advantage of these resources and continue to grow in the career field.  

VT Career and Professional Development [CPD] - provides you with many online resources and guidance, including advising appointments, resume workshops, and much more. Click the icon on the left to access the page. 

Job and Internship Search Guide - filled with helpful tips of where to start looking for internship opportunities and several other available jobs will aid in building workforce experience. Click on the icon to access the page.

Resume and Cover Letter Guide -  packed with information on what resumes and cover letters are along with how to build and format them. Resumes will be key to​ helping you build your future! Click on the icon to access the page.

LinkedIn -  an amazing platform to connect with different individuals in the workforce, but also helps you in applying to several different job positions. To access this page, please click the icon to the left.

Handshake -  another useful tool to that provides numerous job positions to apply to. It's very similar to LinkedIn, but applies more towards Virginia Tech. You can sign up for career events and find mentors! Click on the icon!
